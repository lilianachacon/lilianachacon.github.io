Implementing Nuevo Amanecer To Your Community

The purpose of this guide is to aid community-based organizations in delivering an evidence-based, stress management program for women with breast cancer, called Nuevo Amanecer - A New Dawn. This manual explains the steps necessary to implement a successful program, from hiring personnel, equipment needed, training, program promotion, to evaluation. Community agencies are in the best position to offer the program in a way that fits the needs of local communities.
